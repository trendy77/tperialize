About Us

“We would love to see you and be involved in helping you to look and feel your best!”

Located in Mt Martha, friendly, professional home based and mobile beauty therapy treatments at a reasonable price offering the highest quality service.

Quality treatments include:

EYELASH EXTENSIONS, SPRAY TANNING, WAXING (including XXX), TINTING, SHELLAC/GEL NAILS, MANICURES & PEDICURE (with polish or shellac), MAKE UP (including Bridal) & BASIC HAIR STYLING for all occasions.
All of our services are offered at the highest possible standard whilst still at a reasonable price. We will always do our utmost to be flexible and understand how hectic our lives can be, however we would love to make you feel the best we possibly can so please feel free to Contact Gina at any time to arrange an appointment that is suitable for you.
